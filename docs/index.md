# Umbral

Mass-response engine for *The Fate of Abuc* (POLS399 revolution RPG). The engine
turns each faction's 3-point programme into a calibrated shift in how the
popular constituencies feel and behave, and decides — via hidden Granovetter
thresholds, Epstein active/quiescent dynamics, and Kuran preference
falsification — when a constituency tips and the street turns.
